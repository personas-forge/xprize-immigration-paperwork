## What the live run actually showed (refined model of finding #1)
The naive L1 claim was "digest-only → generic prose." **Opus-low refutes that** — it does not pad with
filler; it marshals every specific that *survives into the digest*, with correct field framing
(composer-as-lead, OSS-as-contribution, athletics-by-its-norms) and **invents nothing**. So the loss is
NOT at the draft step. The live run relocates and sharpens the real mechanism into two confirmed defects:

- **1a — Partial/None criteria are silently suppressed, taking their load-bearing facts with them.**
  buildDraftPrompt writes "ONE section per Met/Strong criterion" (drafting.ts:250), so a strong FACT
  attached to a sub-threshold criterion **vanishes entirely** — not paraphrased, dropped. Ingrid's
  Helsinki Library (filed under Original-contribution:Partial) and Noa's SoundtrackWeekly feature
  (Reviews&press:Partial) both disappeared. Worst for EB-1A final-merits (the totality needs every
  strong fact) and arts (press is load-bearing). The draft has **no path to carry a load-bearing fact
  from a weak criterion into the Conclusion/totality or a stronger section.** ← sharpens #1 + #8.
- **1b — the qualify→digest compression is the lossy boundary, not the draft.** Specifics drop when
  qualify writes the short `evidence` field; the draft can only use what's there. → the fix is upstream:
  richer evidence capture at qualify, and/or feed raw evidence to draft. (The original #1 fix, now
  correctly located on the qualify→draft seam.)
