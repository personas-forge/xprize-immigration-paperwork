## What the drill found (3 real results, not vibes)
1. **The shipped fixes (#115) banked ~$1,235/petition — empirically confirmed.** v0→v1 moved attorney
   rework moderate→light (digest specificity 2,223→2,683 chars; the monograph + dual-office + Helsinki
   year that vanished at v0 now survive). This is the live confirmation that was *pending* after the unit
   tests — the 1b + totality fixes work on a real generation.
2. **Raw-evidence injection (v2) is a DUD — kill the feature.** Dumping the full raw profile on top of the
   1b digest did NOT help and slightly *regressed* (82→76, light→moderate). The "bigger raw-evidence lever"
   I'd flagged as the next investment is **not worth building** — once qualify captures specifics (1b), the
   draft already has what it needs; the raw dump dilutes focus. (Untested: raw *combined with* market
   framing — but v3 wins regardless, so raw is at best redundant.)
3. **Market-bar framing (v3) is the dominant lever — and it's prompt-only.** +18 market points over v2 /
   +12 over the shipped v1, the clear max at **94/100**, driven by field-norm framing (12→18) and explicit
   Kazarian step-two (→19). It's a pure `buildDraftPrompt` change (add: argue the final-merits totality,
   frame every metric against field norms for a lay adjudicator, disciplined comparable-evidence). **This
   is the next thing to ship.**

## Strength confirmed under fire
**Zero fabrication across all four rungs** — including the aggressive market-bar framing AND the raw-data
dump. integrity = 20/20 every time. The no-fabrication discipline holds even when the prompt pushes hard
for persuasive, comparative argument. Protect it (re-test on any future framing change).

## Honest ceilings
- **$ model saturates.** v1 and v3 both score "light rework" → both map to $3,293 in the coarse 4-bucket
  `rework→fraction` map, so the $ column can't see the 82→94 quality gap. The real extra value of v3 shows
  up as (a) quality headroom and (b) **reduced RFE risk** (a step-two, field-normed letter directly
  attacks the most-challenged "original contributions" criterion) — which the value model only prices
  qualitatively. → **refine the value model** (finer rework curve + an explicit RFE-avoidance term).
- **The last ~6 points need DATA, not a prompt.** Even v3's misses are quantitative selectivity comparators
  ("Venice Biennale accepts X%", "N Mies awards/year") that are NOT in the supplied evidence and the model
  must not fabricate. Closing them needs a curated field-norms reference the draft can cite — a feature, a
  bigger investment than a prompt change.
- **One sample per rung.** market_score has stochastic noise (±3); the shape (v3 ≫ rest, v2 dud, v1 > v0)
  is the signal. A rigorous follow-up would run k≥3 per rung.
