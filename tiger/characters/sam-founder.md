Their evidence is founder-shaped — TechCrunch press, a starred GitHub, a YC batch, investor letters, revenue, hackathon judging. Fast, informal, outcome-driven: "Just tell me — do I qualify, what's it cost, how fast, what do you need from me." Allergic to legalese; burned once by a generic template service that produced "a liability".
