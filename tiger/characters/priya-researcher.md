## Who they are
Postdoc-turned-staff-scientist in computational biology on an expiring J-1 with a looming clock. 30+ peer-reviewed papers, an h-index she can recite, citations in the low thousands, two best-paper awards, peer-review and grant-panel service. Precise, citation-minded, slightly wary — "says who, by what standard?" She has watched labmates get O-1 RFEs over sloppy "extraordinary ability" framing, and found ChatGPT confidently wrong about the criteria. Will forgive plainness; will not forgive a confident error.

## Jobs to be done (what they hire the MODEL OUTPUT for)
- An honest read of whether her record clears O-1A, naming *her* evidence not a template — **[[qualify-screening]]**.
- A petition letter mapping her real publications/citations/awards to original-contributions, scholarly articles, judging, awards — **[[draft]]**.
- A point-by-point RFE response crosswalked to the deficient criterion — **[[rfe-response]]** — and an honest read of which criteria are thin / likely to draw an RFE — **[[rfe-forecast]]**.

## Senior-quality bar
At least as good as the draft a strong immigration paralegal would produce after reading her CV — accurate criteria, her real evidence cited, honest about gaps. A senior rejects anything that fabricates, miscategorizes evidence, or asserts a criterion is "Met" without the supporting fact.

## Time-saved (motivation)
LLM-less way = 2–3 months + $5k–$8k (engage a firm, assemble a 500–700-page packet, draft letters). Wins if it collapses drafting weeks to an afternoon *without* lowering the bar; a fast draft she throws away saves nothing.

## Scored acceptance criteria (judged identically every run, applied to the OUTPUT)
- [ ] grounded in MY real context (names her real papers/citations/awards, not placeholders)
- [ ] senior-grade for MY field (the eight criteria + ≥3 threshold represented correctly; nothing she didn't claim shows as "Met")
- [ ] worth the latency/cost
- [ ] zero fabricated awards/citations/publications, and no confident error she'd have to fact-check
- [ ] thin/weak criteria flagged honestly as gaps, not papered over; RFE response references the *original* petition + the specific deficiency
