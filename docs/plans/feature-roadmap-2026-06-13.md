# immigration-paperwork — Feature Roadmap (2026-06-13)
> Generated by Feature Scout (Opus) + triage. Source: Vibeman ideas DB, scan_type=feature_scout.

## Executive Summary
- Ideas generated: 60 · kept after triage: 40 · cut/merged: 20
- Strategic thrust: This roadmap converts a feature-rich demo into a fileable, trustworthy paid product. We harden the money/trust spine (ledger transparency, 402 paywall conversion, durable rate-limiting, consent/audit compliance), then complete the core value chain that makes a petition actually deliverable (persist & export drafts/RFE responses, evidence-gap workflows, readiness scoring, full packet PDF), then turn the attorney/filing workflow from stubs into real e-signature + assignment + sign-off, and finally invest in AI reliability/observability and top-of-funnel growth + the UK second-jurisdiction bet.
- Phase overview:
  - **Phase 1 — Trust, Money & Compliance Spine:** make spend transparent and the paywall convert; close consent/audit/session gaps that block a PII-handling legal product.
  - **Phase 2 — Core Deliverable Value Chain:** stop losing user work and ship the artifacts customers pay for (autosave, DOCX/PDF export, evidence gap tasks, readiness score, packet export).
  - **Phase 3 — Attorney & Filing Workflow:** per-case assignment, sign-off gates, notifications, real e-signature/USCIS filing — the core revenue gate.
  - **Phase 4 — AI Reliability & Observability:** failover, repair-retry, idempotency, CI quality gate, cost/quality dashboards.
  - **Phase 5 — Growth Funnel & Strategic Bets:** lead capture, onboarding intake, screening questionnaire, multi-classification compare, UK Global Talent jurisdiction.

## Phase 1 — Trust, Money & Compliance Spine (make spend transparent, convert the paywall, close the legal/PII gaps)

### Token transaction history: queryable ledger view of every credit, charge, refund and reclaim — [idea: 29bdc99c]
- **Context:** Token Economy & Ledger · **Category:** user_benefit · **Impact/Effort/Risk:** 9/5/2
- **What:** Add `Store.listEntries()` and a paginated history table on `/billing` showing operation label, cost, running balance, timestamp, and reason badges (purchase/draft/refund/signup grant).
- **Why (evidence):** `ledger.ts` is append-only with idempotent credit/charge/refund/reclaim and `registry.labelOf` maps ops to human labels, but `Store` exposes only `getBalance()`; `billing/page.tsx` shows one balance number. The data is written but unreadable.
- **Target files:** `src/lib/token/ledger.ts`, `src/lib/token/registry.ts`, `src/lib/token/store.ts`, `src/app/billing/page.tsx`
- **Depends on:** none (foundation read model)
- **Acceptance criteria:**
  - `listEntries(userId, {limit,cursor})` returns paginated entries with running balance.
  - `/billing` renders the history with human labels and reason badges.
  - Pagination handles >1 page; empty state shown for new users.
